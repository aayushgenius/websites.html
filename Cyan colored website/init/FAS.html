UPPRO is continuously trying to establish FPO as a centre of excellence in areas where it is providing services to small and marginal farmers. At present UPPRO is providing advisory services to 41 FPOs in the state of Uttar Pradesh. The following services are being provided to these FPOs:



Hand holding support in day-to-day business operations.
Ensuring better governance and leadership.
Capacity building of FPO Board members and FPO staff.
Designing FPO specific product and services which can leverage required services to farmers members.
