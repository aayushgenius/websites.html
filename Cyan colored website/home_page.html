Our Vision

Enhancing small and marginal farmers’ livelihood through technology and improve profitability. Improve the livelihoods of small and marginalized farmers through market and technology-based interventions

Our Mission

Provide end-to-end services in agriculture and allied products to aid farmers achieve a self-sustaining and respectful livelihood.

Our Objective

To undertake policy advocacy, explore wider market and strategic partnership for FPOs

To facilitate FPO’s for carrying out all agriculture extension activities for benefit of farmers.

Procure & Supply agri inputs like seed, fertilizer, manure, pesticide, micro-nutrient etc to the FPO’s at economic rates, timely and at doorstep.

To create infrastructure for procuring, storing, grading and processing of agriculture produce of
the FPO’s members and support the FPO’s for marketing through direct purchase, linkage and
value addition.

Providing extension services in close coordination with the Agriculture and other line departments for reaching the benefits to the small and marginal farmers.

To Promote Pulses, Vegetables and Dairy Cluster under different projects extended by
government/other agency.
