HarvestPlus

Harvest Plus leads a global effort to improve nutrition and public health by developing and disseminating staple food crops rich in vitamins and minerals. Harvest Plus is part of the CGIAR research program on Agriculture for Nutrition and Health. Harvest Plus improves nutrition and public health in India by promoting pearl millet that provides more iron and wheat that contains more zinc in the diet. Harvest Plus and its partners work in five states of India to improve the availability, adoption, and consumption of biofortified pearl millet and wheat. The goal is that more than 1 million Indian farming households will be growing these crops by 2018.


Harvest Plus with the help of UPPRO implementing project on replication and distribution of Zn fortified wheat seed and Iron fortified Perl Millet. The project spread is in 6 districts of UP.
