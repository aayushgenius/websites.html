Who We Are

Connecting Farmers to the Market
UPPRO Kisan Producer Company Limited is a federation of FPOs of small and marginal farmers operational in different districts of Uttar Pradesh.
Registered under the Indian Company Act, 2013 (Producer Company under Section 581a in Part IX A of the Company Act, 1956), UPPRO has been established with the support of Small Farmer's Agri-Business Consortium, a society promoted by the Dept. Of Agriculture, Govt. Of India.
Through digitization and technological intervention, UPPRO aims to connect small and marginalized farmers to the market and help make agriculture a sustainable source of livelihood.

UPPRO is working to provide end-to-end services in agriculture and allied product to improve profitability.
In the next three years it hopes to:

1. Undertake policy advocacy, explore the broader market, and strategic partnerships for FPOs.
2. Facilitate FPO's to carry out all agriculture extension activities for the benefit of farmers.
3. Procure & supply agricultural inputs like seeds, fertilizers, manure, pesticides, and micro-nutrient to FPOâ€™s at economical rates
4. Create infrastructure for procuring, storing, grading, and processing agriculture produce of the FPO members and support the marketing efforts of FPOs through direct purchase, linkage, and value addition.
5. Provide extension services in close coordination with the Agriculture and other line departments for reaching the benefits to the small and marginal farmers.
6. Promote Pulses, Vegetables, and Dairy Cluster under different projects extended by government/other agencies.
