Technology Intervention

Linking farmers with the technology to create competitive advantage has always been a prime objective for UPPRO. On the technology front, following interventions have been done :

  1. Digtization of FPOs

Most Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO) are relatively new companies and tend to
operate using manual processes and methods. Day-to-day operations of FPOs require
co-ordination between the shareholder farmers, supporting institutions and other players in the ecosystem like buyers, suppliers and logistics operators. The speed of operations and successful scale-up of an FPO is heavily impacted by the number of people it can hire to manage the daily co-ordination, office and field operations.

Typical FPOs are limited to operate with usually two or three dedicated employees to minimize the cost of operations. Further manual recordkeeping and maintaining multiple record books increases the chances of error and makes reconciliation across business transactions difficult. This makes the case for simple digital tools for FPOs which can help improve the operational efficiency of the FPO workforce and also aid in digitally tracking all business transactions in an easy and secure manner while digitizing the entire business, operations, and financial activities of an FPO.

UPPRO has undertaken FPO digitization with the following objectives:

  1. . To increase efficiency, effectiveness and decision making in FPO business operation using technology platform. This will mainly include Shareholder data management, Inputs Stock and Sales tracking, Shareholder Crops and Outputs Sales tracking, FPO Business and operational metrics dashboards.


  2. To ensure technology intervention will bring efficient, faster and cost effective services for farmer members


Total 2 FPOs have been fully digitized with the help of CDFI (Centre for Digital Financial Inclusion, Delhi ). Work is going on in 3 more FPOs to digitize their business operations. The following activities have been completed in FPO digitization:

Farm mechanization



UPPRO facilitated FPOs in establishing a custom hiring bank /farm machinery bank. 36 FPOs have so far been facilitated in establishing a farm machinery bank The objectives of this intervention has been:

  • Enhancement of timeliness and profitability in farm operations


  • Provision of good finishes for manually unattainable farm operations


  • Reduction of boredom and drudgery in farm operations


  • Reduction of labour requirements and enhancement of agricultural production through higher rates of work output
