Financial Services

Arranging finance to meet the working capital for running FPO business has prime agenda for UPPRO. In this regards, UPPRO helped FPOs members in the following ways :



  1. SFAC equity matching grants: 36 FPOs have been helped to avail SFAC equity matching grants. A total amount of Rs 144 lakh has been generated with the help of SFAC equity matching grants.


  2. Establishing Financial linkages: Total 8 FPOs have been helped in getting cash credit linkages and credit facilities. Major institution linkages have been established with Bank of Baroda, Oriental Bank of Commerce, Ananya Finance.
