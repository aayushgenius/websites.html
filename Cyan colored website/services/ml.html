Market Linkages

For increasing the farmers’ income, market linkages has always been of utmost importance. In this regard, UPPRO has, by and large, succeeded in providing better market linkages to member-farmers. However, the organization still faces a host of challenges. Following interventions have been done on this front :



  1. IFFCO-IIFDC Krishi Sewa Kendras: To ensure the quality, cost-effectiveness and timely supply of agricultural inputs at the farmers’ doorstep, UPPRO has helped FPOs in establishing linkages with IFFCO. 40 FPOs now have established IFFCO-IIFDC Krishi Sewa Kendras. Presently a total number of 21200 farmers members are getting services from these Centers.



  2. Agri-produce market linkages: Market linkage for Mangoes UP is knows for Dashari mangoes. Last year, UPPRO helped 3 FPOs in establishing market linkages for Mangoes. A total 98 quintals of mangoes were given market linkages with institutional tieups with Reliance Fresh & Big Basket. During the COVID 19 pandemic, when even institutional tie-ups were not possible, UPPRO initiated online marketing of mangoes. A total of 25 Quintals of mangoes were sold online.
